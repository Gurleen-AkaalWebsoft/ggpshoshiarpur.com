Guru Gobind Singh Public School, Hoshiarpur

Learning Today, Leading Tomorrow!

Guru Gobind Singh Public School (GGPS), Hoshiarpur is a distinguished institution dedicated to nurturing young minds into confident, capable, and compassionate individuals. Established in October 2018 under the aegis of the Guru Gobind Educational Society (GGES), GGPS began with just 15 Pre-Nursery students. Today, it proudly educates over 1,600 students, standing as one of the leading educational institutions in Hoshiarpur.
GGPS is committed to providing value-based, quality education that combines academic excellence with character development. Our approach emphasizes collaborative learning, critical thinking, problem-solving, and real-world experiences, ensuring students are actively engaged in their educational journey.
With innovative teaching methods, modern classrooms, and a focus on extracurricular engagement, we strive to shape globally aware, socially responsible, and academically strong individuals ready to lead with confidence.
Join GGPS on this inspiring path of growth and learning, where every child is empowered to discover their potential and excel with integrity and purpose.
