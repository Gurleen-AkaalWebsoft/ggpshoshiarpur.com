Get To Know Us

Leading The Best Digital School

“Our vision is to offer an articulated pedagogy that empowers students to be independent critical thinkers and responsible global leaders.” Guru Gobind Singh Public School (GGPS), Hoshiarpur, stands as a beacon of excellence in education, nurturing young minds to become confident, compassionate, and competent individuals. Founded in October 2018 under the aegis of Guru Gobind Educational Society (GGES), GGPS began its journey with just 15 Pre-Nursery students. Since then, the school has grown remarkably and is now home to over 1600 students, making it one of the top educational institutions in Hoshiarpur. Our school is committed to delivering value-based, quality education that blends academic rigor with character building. At GGPS, we foster an environment where collaborative learning, critical thinking, problem-solving, and real-world experiences are integral to the educational process. Through innovative teaching methods, well-equipped classrooms, and engaging extracurricular activities, we aim to develop globally aware, socially responsible, and academically strong individuals ready to meet the challenges of tomorrow. Join us on this inspiring journey of learning and growth where every child is nurtured to discover their potential and soar high with confidence and integrity.
