Safe Transport through GPS Systemg

To ensure the safety and security of our students during their commute, our school has implemented a modern GPS-based transport tracking system. This system allows real-time monitoring of school buses, ensuring timely updates and complete visibility of routes and stops. Parents and school authorities can track the exact location of the buses through a mobile app or web portal, receive alerts for pick-up and drop-off times, and be informed of any delays or route changes instantly.
By using GPS technology, we aim to enhance student safety, improve communication, and provide peace of mind to parents and guardians. Our commitment is to make every journey to and from school as secure and efficient as possible.
Our school prioritizes the safety of every child, especially during daily travel. To enhance security and improve communication, we have introduced a GPS-enabled school transport system. This advanced technology allows for real-time tracking of all school buses, ensuring parents and school staff are always informed about the location and status of each vehicle.

Rules & Regulations

  • The bus Pass should be carried by the student always when using the bus facility.

  • Anyone found travelling by the school buses without valid pass will be asked to pay the bus fare for the entire month.

  • If a child needs to avail a trip other than the scheduled one prior permission of the vice principal or principal is required.

  • Students availing bus facility are expected to be on the proper side of their bus stop at least five minutes prior to the scheduled time of the arrival of the bus.

  • Students waiting for the buses should stay away from the main road until the bus arrives.

  • The Buses will not wait for late corners.

  • No bus rider should come near the entry door of the bus until the bus has come to a complete stop.

  • All bus riders must occupy vacant seats immediately after boarding.

  • The drivers are authorized to stop buses at the designated stops only, unless otherwise directed by the Bus Incharge. The list of stops will be prepared keeping in view the convenience and satisfaction of bus riders and is subjected to change.

  • When the bus is in motion, student must not move around in the bus. The riders must not have any part of their body out of the windows.

  • Nobody is allowed to eat in the bus.

  • The front door of the bus is only permissible for entrance and exit.

  • Bus Pass will not be cancelled for the students of std XII after the month of July for current academic year.

  • Object of any kind most not be discarded inside or thrown out of the bus.

  • Students will be held responsible for any damage to the buses caused by their negligence or deliberate act.

  • Shrieking, shouting or any unruly behavior Is never expected from the students.

  • Shrieking, shouting or any unruly behavior Is never expected from the students.

  • The teachers in the bus, students and bus monitors are responsible for maintaining discipline in the buses and any violation of rules should be reported to the Principal.

  • The transport in-charge is to be consulted for details of bus routes.
