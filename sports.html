Where Every Game Builds Character and Confidence

At GGPS, we believe that education is incomplete without physical fitness, teamwork, and a spirit of healthy competition. Our school places a strong emphasis on sports and physical education as an integral part of overall student development. Through well-structured sports programs, we aim to nurture discipline, resilience, leadership, and a never-give-up attitude in every student.
At GGPS, our library stands as the heart of our learning environment—a vibrant space where curiosity meets knowledge. More than just a room of books, it’s a hub of exploration, creativity, and lifelong learning.
Our campus features excellent sports facilities, including well-maintained fields and courts for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, athletics, and indoor games. Professional coaches and physical education instructors guide students with proper training and techniques, ensuring both fun and skill-building go hand in hand. Regular practice sessions, inter-house competitions, and participation in inter-school and district-level tournaments help students shine at every level.
Sports at GGPS is not just about winning medals—it’s about building character, embracing teamwork, and learning the values of fairness and respect. Whether it’s a morning drill, a team huddle before a match, or celebrating a hard-fought game, every moment on the field contributes to shaping confident, balanced, and physically active individuals ready to face life with energy and enthusiasm.
